A journey, left to right

Lateral

Twenty-eight years of film, unrolled into a single horizontal banner. Travel sideways through the places — and the faces — a Leica was pointed at, from a wheat field in Tuscany to a café table in Paris.

01Italy43.3°N 11.3°E

Tuscany

Wheat and patchwork hills, and the four-minute hour when the whole country turns the colour of a struck match.

02USA36.3°N 121.9°W

The Pacific

Cold water before the world is awake. The surface is never the subject — only what it agrees, for a moment, to show you.

03Iceland · Norway · Canada64°N

The North

Slow film and slower water. A quarter-second draws the falls into one continuous white gesture; silence does the rest.

04Japan35.0°N 135.8°E

Kyoto

One frame, backlit. The light does not arrive in Kyoto — it filters, already softened, through the leaves.

05Lisbon · Havana · MarrakeshPORTRAITS

Faces

A little warmer than the truth. I have never wanted my portraits to be accurate — only fair.

06Paris · Havana48.8°N 2.3°E

Cities

Café tables and Sunday drives. The last light of the banner, where the road bends home.
