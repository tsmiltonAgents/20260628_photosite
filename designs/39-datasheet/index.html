Notes — General

Each frame is annotated as it was scanned. Dimension lines report the true pixel extent of the negative scan; crop guides indicate the rule-of-thirds composition; the exposure callout marks the metered focal plane. No frame was cropped after exposure.

Tolerances

Linear ±2 px · Exposure ±⅓ EV · Colour graded to approximate a Kodak Portra print. Monochrome frames left as exposed on Ilford stock.
