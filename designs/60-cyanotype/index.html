Impressiones Solis — Thomae Milton, fecit

A complete folio of the Grain archive, re-imagined in the manner of Anna Atkins: each photograph contact-printed as a Prussian-blue cyanotype, deep iron-blue against the white of cold-pressed paper. Exposed by daylight, washed in water, dried in the open air.
